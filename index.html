About Us
MC Nails is a local nail salon based in Fairfax, VA. We have been in business since 2009. We started out with two people, but since then, we have grown into something bigger. Despite the many changes that MC Nails have been through, our priority have always been to satisfy the customers. We've earned the trust of many local as well as non-local customers, and along the way we've even made good friends. Come in and try us out!
